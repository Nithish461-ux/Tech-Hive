# Scholarships and Financial Aid

## Merit Scholarships
Merit scholarships up to 100% tuition fee are available for exceptionally talented students. These are awarded based on entrance exam scores and board examination marks.

## Categories of Scholarships

### Full Scholarships (100% Tuition)
- Top 5% students in merit list
- Covers tuition fee, examination fee, and library fee
- Free hostel accommodation
- Monthly stipend of Rs. 2,000

### 75% Scholarship
- Top 10% students in merit list
- Covers 75% of tuition fee
- Free hostel accommodation
- Monthly stipend of Rs. 1,500

### 50% Scholarship
- Top 15% students in merit list
- Covers 50% of tuition fee
- Free hostel accommodation if needed

### 25% Scholarship
- Top 20% students in merit list
- Covers 25% of tuition fee

## Special Category Scholarships

### SC/ST Scholarships
- Students belonging to Scheduled Castes or Scheduled Tribes
- 50% to 100% scholarship based on merit within category
- Reservation as per government norms

### Sports Scholarships
- Available for recognized sports
- Percentage based on level of achievement (state/national)
- Additional sports training facility access
- Priority hostel accommodation

### Need-based Aid
- Financial assistance for economically disadvantaged students
- Maximum 50% of tuition fee
- Based on family income and financial circumstances
- Renewable annually based on academic performance

## Post-Matric Scholarship
- Government sponsored scheme for students from minority communities
- Covers tuition and examination fees
- Monthly subsistence allowance

## Research Scholarships
- Available for M.Tech and M.Sc students pursuing research
- Covers stipend and research expenses
- Based on academic merit and research proposal quality

## Application Process
1. Merit scholarships are automatically awarded based on admission merit list
2. Need-based aid requires submission of financial documents
3. Sports scholarships require sports trial and verification
4. Application deadlines mentioned in admission letter
5. All scholarships valid for duration of program with minimum CGPA requirement

## Renewal Criteria
- Maintain minimum CGPA of 3.5 for full scholarships
- Minimum CGPA of 3.0 for 75% and 50% scholarships
- No disciplinary action or malpractice records
- Regular attendance as per institutional requirements
- Good conduct and behavioral standards

## Scholarship Benefits
- Direct fee deposit to institutional account
- Hostel accommodation (where applicable)
- Monthly stipend for living expenses
- Waiver of various institutional fees
- Priority in research assistant positions
- Preference in internship placements

## Appeal and Review
- Students can appeal for scholarship review
- Annual review of scholarship continuation
- Case-by-case consideration for exceptional circumstances
- Committee for scholarship grievance resolution
