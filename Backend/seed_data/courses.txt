# Academic Programs and Courses

Our institution offers 50+ undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines.

## Undergraduate Programs
- Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science, Information Technology
- Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) in Accounting, Finance, Business Administration
- Bachelor of Arts (B.A) in English, History, Economics, Political Science, Psychology
- Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science

## Postgraduate Programs
- Master of Science (M.Sc) in various scientific disciplines
- Master of Business Administration (MBA)
- Master of Arts (M.A)
- Master of Technology (M.Tech)

## Core Curriculum
Students in Science stream study Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects along with specialized courses in their chosen discipline.

Commerce students focus on Accounting, Economics, Business Law, and Taxation among others.

Arts students have flexibility in choosing subjects from Humanities and Social Sciences.

Engineering students follow a rigorous curriculum combining theory and practical applications in their respective fields.

## Credit System
All programs follow a semester-based credit system. Each course carries specific credit hours. Students need to complete minimum credit hours to graduate.

## Electives and Specializations
Students can choose electives based on their interests. The institution offers specialization tracks in areas like Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Finance, Marketing, and many others.

## Faculty
Our faculty members are experienced professionals with advanced degrees and industry experience. The student-faculty ratio is maintained at 15:1 for effective learning.

## Laboratory Facilities
All science and engineering departments have well-equipped laboratories with modern instruments and software for hands-on learning experiences.
