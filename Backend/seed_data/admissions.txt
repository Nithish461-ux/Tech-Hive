# Admissions Information

## Admission Process
Admission to our institution is based on merit and entrance exam scores. The selection process ensures that we enroll talented and dedicated students.

## Application Deadline
Application deadline is usually in June every year. Students are advised to submit their applications well before the deadline as seats are limited and filled on a first-come, first-served basis after merit consideration.

## Entrance Examinations
- National-level entrance exams like JEE, NEET are accepted for engineering and science programs
- CAT/MAT scores for MBA program
- State-level entrance exams for commerce and arts programs

## Required Documents
- 10+2 mark sheet
- Entrance exam score card
- Valid ID proof
- Address proof
- Category certificate (if applicable)
- Medical fitness certificate

## Merit List
Merit lists are published based on entrance exam scores and board examination marks. Students are shortlisted based on their combined merit score.

## Counseling and Seat Allocation
Shortlisted students attend counseling sessions where they choose their preferred program and branch based on merit and availability.

## Fee Payment
Selected students must pay the admission fee and other charges within the specified date to confirm their seat. Fee payment can be done online or through bank transfer.

## Hostel Allocation
Students seeking hostel accommodation apply during the admission process. Hostel seats are allocated based on merit and priority given to outstation students.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Passed 10+2 or equivalent with minimum required marks
- Valid entrance exam score
- No age limit for most programs
- Physical and mental fitness as per institutional norms
