# Placements and Career Services

## Placement Statistics
- 100+ companies recruit from our campus annually
- Average placement package: 6-12 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum)
- Highest package: 25 LPA in recent placement season
- Lowest package: 3.5 LPA
- 95% overall placement rate

## Companies Recruiting
- Top IT companies: TCS, Infosys, Cognizant, Wipro, HCL
- Finance sector: ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, Goldman Sachs
- Consulting firms: McKinsey, Boston Consulting Group, Deloitte
- E-commerce: Amazon, Flipkart, OYO
- And many others from various sectors

## Recruitment Process
- On-campus interviews conducted during placement season (November-March)
- Companies conduct pre-placement talks and coding contests
- Multiple rounds: Written test, Group discussion, Technical interview, HR interview
- Offer letters issued on same day after final interview

## Placement Criteria
- Minimum CGPA: 7.0 (may vary for different companies)
- No active backlog at time of placement
- English proficiency requirement
- Communication and interpersonal skills
- Domain-specific technical knowledge

## Career Services
- Career counseling available for all students
- Resume writing workshops
- Interview preparation training
- Group discussion coaching
- Technical aptitude enhancement programs
- Soft skills development
- Mock interviews and mock placement tests

## Alumni Network
- Strong alumni network across sectors and geographies
- Alumni mentorship program
- Industry connections and networking events
- Alumni guest lectures sharing industry insights

## Internship Program
- Summer internships (May-July) with corporate companies
- Stipend typically Rs. 15,000-25,000 per month
- Credits and evaluation based on internship performance
- Helps in skill development and industry exposure
- Many internships convert to full-time offers

## Higher Studies
- Guidance for MS and PhD programs in India and abroad
- GATE exam preparation
- GRE/GMAT coaching available
- Information about scholarship opportunities
- Liaison with foreign universities

## Placement Policy
- No discrimination based on gender, caste, or religion
- Consecutive offer rule: Students can accept better offers if available
- Two job offers per student limit in campus season
- Ethical recruitment practices enforced
- Student protection against unfair practices
