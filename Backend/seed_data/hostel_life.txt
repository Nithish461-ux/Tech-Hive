# Hostel and Campus Life

## Hostel Facilities
We provide residential facilities for about 60% of our student population. Separate hostels for boys and girls with modern amenities.

## Hostel Features
- Twin-sharing and triple-sharing rooms available
- Attached bathrooms with hot water facility
- Individual lockers for secure storage
- High-speed WiFi in all rooms
- Study tables and chairs
- LED lighting and AC/Fan in rooms
- Laundry service twice a week
- 24-hour security with CCTV surveillance

## Hostel Mess
- Nutritious meals provided three times daily
- Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options
- Special dietary meal on request (subject to approval)
- Mess fee: Rs. 2,000-3,000 per month depending on meal plan
- Monthly menu planned by nutritionist
- Mess committee for grievance resolution

## Hostel Rules and Regulations
- Curfew timings: 10 PM on weekdays, 11 PM on weekends
- Guests allowed with warden permission only
- No smoking or drinking on hostel premises
- Ragging strictly prohibited with zero tolerance policy
- Noise restrictions after 10 PM
- Electricity and water usage norms to be followed

## Hostel Discipline
- Strict action for violation of rules
- Fine up to Rs. 1,000 for breaches
- Expulsion from hostel for serious violations like ragging
- Appeal process available for students

## Hostel Applications
- Online application during admission process
- Priority given to outstation students
- Distance from home criteria considered
- Allocation based on merit and merit number

## Campus Activities
- Regular sports tournaments and cultural events
- Debate and discussion forums
- Art and craft workshops
- Yoga and fitness classes
- Music and dance rehearsals
- Movie screening nights
- Guest lectures and seminars

## Campus Amenities
- Multi-purpose sports complex with basketball, volleyball courts
- Cricket and football grounds
- Outdoor gymnasium
- Swimming pool (seasonal)
- Amphitheater for events
- Cafeteria and snack bars
- Medical clinic with resident doctor
- ATM and bank counter
- Post office and parcel facility
- Student activity center

## Additional Services
- Internet connectivity: Free WiFi with 50 Mbps speed
- Laundry service: Rs. 10 per kg
- Room cleaning: Twice a week
- Power backup for 8 hours
- Emergency medical assistance 24/7
