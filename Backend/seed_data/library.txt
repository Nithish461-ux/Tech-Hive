# Library Services and Information

## Library Hours
- Weekdays: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM (Monday to Friday)
- Weekends: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Saturday and Sunday)
- Holidays: Closed

## Collection
Our library has 50,000+ books and extensive digital resources covering all subject areas offered in the institution.

## Categories
- General Reference: 5,000 books
- Science and Mathematics: 8,000 books
- Engineering and Technology: 10,000 books
- Commerce and Business: 6,000 books
- Arts and Humanities: 7,000 books
- Special Collections: 4,000 books
- E-books: 10,000+ titles
- Journals: 200+ subscriptions
- Digital Databases: 50+ databases

## Book Issue and Return Policy
- Students can issue maximum 3 books at a time
- Issue period: 2 weeks for regular books, 1 week for reference books
- Late fine: Rs. 5 per day per book
- Reference books: Library use only, cannot be issued
- Digital resources: Unlimited access during library hours

## Library Facilities
- Reading rooms with 200+ seats
- Computer lab with 40 systems for research
- WiFi throughout library premises
- Photocopying and scanning services
- Printing facilities
- Discussion rooms for group study
- Individual study carrels
- Audio-visual resources room

## Online Access
Students can access digital resources remotely using their student ID and password. E-books, journals, and databases are available 24/7 online.

## Librarian Services
- Reference desk for research assistance
- User education programs
- Interlibrary loan services
- Citation and referencing guidance
- Research database training workshops

## Member Registration
Students automatically get library membership upon enrollment. Faculty and staff can register separately. Valid ID required for borrowing privileges.

## Membership Benefits
- Access to all library resources
- Free WiFi access
- Discounted photocopying rates
- Access to online databases
- Borrowing privileges for books
